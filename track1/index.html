The Ram

Aries: you’re first on the list

So you’re first to take on the world.

Taking control of the situation and spontaneity is what you’re known for.

You’re determined, devoted, bold, and encouraging.

You love a challenge, and when given one,

You’re basically

Unstoppable

But is your reckless nature dangerous?

You want to take control and be the best but you at times forget those around you.

You want to shatter the glass ceiling but sometimes don’t mind the glass falling on those below you.

Your energy commands the room, but you command others.

Your confidence is a virtue that the other signs can learn from and your originality is commendable.

But remember to sometimes slow down with the others around you,

As your movements are too fast for the rest of the world.
